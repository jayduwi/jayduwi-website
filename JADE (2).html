ABOUT ME

My full name is Jade Louie M. Lalu; however, most people call me by my nicknames, such as Jade, Louie, Hiyas, or Jay Uwi. I came from the town of Mapaniqui in Candaba, Pampanga. My family is small but close-knit, as it consists of only four people: my father, Jeffrey M. Lalu, my mother, Lory M. Lalu, my younger brother, Jelo Maverick M. Lalu, and myself. My motto has always been "you can’t control the wind, but you can adjust your sail," reminding me that you can’t control the things around you, but it’s the way you react and decide what you will do to go with it.
